    5. Ensure `sys.stdout` is reconfigured for UTF-8 and the stream handler respects the `verbose` flag (DEBUG if True, INFO if False).

  </verify>
  <done>Logging is provisioned with a singleton context and aggressive noise suppression based on the verbose flag.</done>
