## 4. `tests/test_semantic_grouping.py`
**Role:** Validates the semantic grouping logic locally with mocked Pass 1 results.
**Data Flow:** Provides lists of `PageClassification` instances with pre-written edge-case summaries to `_group_pages_into_documents` to verify that the logic intelligently branches, merges, and carries over context boundaries (e.g., blank or signature-only pages).
