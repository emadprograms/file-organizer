## Decisions Captured

### Module Entrypoints
- **Decision**: Keep fuzzy clustering and LLM matching as separate functions.
- **Details**: `name_matcher.py` will expose `cluster_names_fuzzily` and `canonicalize_with_llm` independently. `pipeline.py` will continue to call them sequentially.
- **Reasoning**: User preference for maintaining modularity and explicit steps in the orchestrator.

### Timeline Generation
- **Decision**: Move `build_tenant_timelines` to the `timeline/` module.
- **Details**: Relocate the timeline-building logic out of `tenants.py` and into the existing `timeline/` directory to consolidate date/timeline logic.
- **Reasoning**: Keeps the architecture clean, aligns perfectly with the Phase 16 refactoring boundaries, and effectively cleans out `tenants.py` as a "dumping ground."
